# 📌 Project Overview

The **Online Sales Dashboard** is a Business Intelligence project developed using **Microsoft Power BI** to analyze and visualize online sales data. The project converts raw sales data into meaningful and interactive reports that help businesses monitor performance, identify trends, and make informed decisions.

The dashboard provides an easy-to-understand view of important business metrics such as **Sales, Profit, Quantity, Customer Performance, Product Categories, and Regional Analysis**. Interactive charts, KPI cards, and filters allow users to explore the data efficiently and gain valuable business insights.

---

# 🎯 Project Objectives

### 1️⃣ Analyze Customer Purchasing Behavior

Analyze customer buying patterns and purchasing trends to better understand customer preferences and improve business strategies.

### 2️⃣ Evaluate Sales Performance

Monitor key business metrics such as **Total Sales, Profit, Revenue, Orders, and Quantity Sold** to measure overall business performance.

### 3️⃣ Compare Regional and Product Performance

Compare sales across different regions and product categories to identify top-performing markets and products while recognizing areas that need improvement.

### 4️⃣ Build an Interactive Dashboard

Develop a user-friendly dashboard using **Power BI** that allows users to explore business data through interactive charts, KPI cards, and filters.

### 5️⃣ Support Data-Driven Decision Making

Provide meaningful insights that help stakeholders optimize sales strategies, improve customer satisfaction, and make informed business decisions.

---

# ❗ Problem Statement

Businesses generate a large amount of sales data every day. Analyzing this data manually is time-consuming and often leads to delayed decision-making. Without proper visualization, it becomes difficult to monitor sales trends, compare regional performance, identify profitable products, and understand customer behavior.

---

# 💡 Solution

This project solves these challenges by creating an interactive dashboard using **Power BI**. The data was cleaned and transformed using **Power Query** before designing visualizations. The dashboard enables users to analyze sales performance using dynamic charts, graphs, KPI cards, and slicers.
